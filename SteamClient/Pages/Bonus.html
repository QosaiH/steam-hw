Recomended Games

Example of usage:
Input: "This game is amazing, I loved every second of it!" Output: Sentiment: Positive (99.99%)
Input: "The game is buggy and not worth the money." Output: Sentiment: Negative (99.98%)
What are the use cases?
The model is useful in a system where users write reviews of games, and it allows for:
Rapid identification of overall feelings from feedback.
Ability to analyze trends (how many positive or negative reviews exist).
